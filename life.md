# On Life

> Vinoba: "*Brahma satyam jagat sphurtih jivanam satya shodhanam.*"
> "Brahman is truth, the world its vibrant manifestation and life is truth's pursuit."

> Vinoba: "It is a search after this ever growing reality and its realisation."

> It is this new approach which gives us a basic principle for unity of theory and practice. This principle provides also for his social philosophy and activities like Bhudan, Gramdan, ...
