# Action

> Mendicants, there are these three sources that give rise to deeds. (...) Greed, hate, and delusion. (...) Any deed that emerges from greed, hate, or delusion — born, sourced, and originated from greed, hate, or delusion — is unskillful, blameworthy, results in suffering, and leads to the creation of more deeds, not their cessation. (...)

> There are these three sources that give rise to deeds. Contentment, love, and understanding (...). Any deed that emerges from contentment, love, or understanding —born, sourced, and originated from contentment, love, or understanding — is skillful, blameless, results in happiness, and leads to the cessation of more deeds, not their creation.

```
       Action = Intention
         ______|______
        |             |
    skillfull      unskillful
       |               |
    Contentment     Greed
    Love            Hate/Aversion
    Understanding   Delusion

```
